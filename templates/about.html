About Our Project

This project is an AI-powered Pneumonia Detection System designed to assist doctors and patients by analyzing chest X-ray images. With the power of Machine Learning, we aim to provide quick, reliable, and efficient preliminary diagnosis to improve healthcare accessibility.

AI Technology

We leverage Deep Learning to analyze chest X-rays with high accuracy and reliability.

Doctor Friendly

Designed to assist radiologists and pulmonologists with quick decision-making tools.

Patient Focused

Our system ensures easy accessibility for patients, bridging gaps in healthcare services.
