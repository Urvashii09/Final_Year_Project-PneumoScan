About Our Pneumonia Detection App

This application leverages advanced AI and deep learning models to accurately detect pneumonia from chest X-ray images. The final objective is to integrate the trained model into a full-stack web application that allows users to upload chest X-ray images and receive predictions regarding the presence of pneumonia. The application will also include features such as real-time analysis, intuitive user interface, medical report generation, and history tracking. This makes the system practical and ready for deployment in real-world clinical environments or as a diagnostic aid for medical professionals.
